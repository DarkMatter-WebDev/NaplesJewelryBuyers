FAQ

Common Questions

How do you determine what you'll pay?
+

We base our offers on the current live spot price of gold or silver, the karat purity, and the weight of your items. We test and weigh everything transparently in front of you so you can see exactly how we arrive at our number.

Do I need an appointment?
+

Yes — a quick call or text is all it takes. We'll schedule a time that works for you and come to your home, office, or any location you prefer in the Naples area.

What condition do my items need to be in?
+

Condition often does not matter for gold and silver — the precious metal value is the same whether a piece is broken, bent, or tarnished. Bring us everything and let us evaluate it.

What if I don't know the karat or purity?
+

No problem at all. We bring professional testing equipment with us and can determine the exact purity and value of any item on the spot, at no charge to you.

Is there any obligation to sell?
+

Absolutely not. Our visit and appraisal are completely free. You can accept our offer, decline it, or take a few days to think about it — no pressure, no hard feelings.

How quickly will I be paid?
+

If you accept our offer, you are paid immediately in cash on the spot. There are no checks to wait for or bank transfers to track.
