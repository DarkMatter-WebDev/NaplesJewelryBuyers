How to Tell Sterling Silver from Silver Plate

Look for marks such as “sterling,” “925,” “.925,” or “999.” Many older items are marked differently, and silver-plated pieces can look like solid sterling silver. If you are unsure, set it aside for evaluation rather than guessing. Clear photos of hallmarks, patterns, and any maker’s marks are especially helpful.
