Before you sell a coin collection

Do not clean coins or remove them from holders. Cleaning can reduce collector value. Keep any labels, boxes, receipts, grading certificates, and collection notes with the items. If you send photos first, include clear images of both sides, dates, mint marks, holders, and the overall collection.
